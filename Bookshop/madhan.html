Vairamuthu Poem

Vairamuthu is one of the leading writer in tamil nadu.it was vairamuthu's one of the book

Bhagavathgeetha

He tries to get united with God all the time so that through him but in his ignorance no vice or evil or improper thing or work is done which may bind his soul to this earth through rebirth. Such persons are called mahatmas by the Lord in the B.G. Look for one who practices yog.

Agni siragugal

Wings of fire tell that an ordinary person can also achieve everything he dream & he deserve in life only if he has the determination, if he has the courage to overcome the failures, & if he is strong enough to face the struggles.
